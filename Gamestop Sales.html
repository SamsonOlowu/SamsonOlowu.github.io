Gamestop Sales Performance

Data Visualization using Looker Studio

Project Summary

For this project, Looker Studio was employed to create insightful visualizations focused on analyzing sales and performance data for Gamestop. The report encompassed various aspects of the company's sales performance, including top-performing brands, product type unit sales and rating correlation, inventory analysis, and category performance.

Tools

Looker Studio

Insights

- The game'MLB The Show 19' emergd as the best-selling product with a total sale of 2,845 units sold.
- The brand Brydge generated the highest revenue for Gamestop, selling 583 units yielding $75,784 in revenue.
- Two categories 'Super Nitendo' and 'Accessories & Watches' achieved a perfect rating of 5.0(100%).
